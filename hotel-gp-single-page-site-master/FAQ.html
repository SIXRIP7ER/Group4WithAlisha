FAQ

How do I search for rooms by price?

In order to do so, head over to the side navigation bar and the rooms are sorted by memebership types. The cheapest of memberships being the Bronze and upwards towards the Gold Membership. The booking page also displays the rooms types in ascending order of price from left to right.

How do I navigate to the booking page?

Our main page has a button "BOOK NOW" which will direct you to the booking page. Another alternative is to click on the side navigation bar to the left and click "Booking Page" and this will take you to the booking page where you can book a stay for your pet!

Do you offer any special discounts?

Discounts are usually provided on a seasonal basis. During our peak trading periods such as Christmas, New Year, and public holidays, we have promotions dependant on the type and duration of your pet's stay. Please follow us on social media to keep up to the latest promotions.

How do I submit a complaint?

We accept complaints through email and over the phone, where you will be redirected to the hotel manager. Head over to our contact us page for further information regarding reaching out to us.
