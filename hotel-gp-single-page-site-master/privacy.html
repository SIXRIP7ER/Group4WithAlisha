Privacy Policy

BestMate Paradise will maintain the privacy of the information it collects and its compliance with the Privacy Act and the Australian Privacy Principles.

Collection of Personal Information

  • Personal Informaton entered by users such as Name, Email Address, Phone Number, and Personal Address will not be shared to any other third parties and shall only be used for recording purposes at BestMate Paradise.

  • BestMate Paradise has the right to collect informations used as a part of visiting the website including but is not limited to IP addresses, location informations and cookies.

Security of Information

Personal Information relating to an account will be stored securely on BestMate Paradise's database until the personal accounts are deleted and thus completely removing all informations relating to it.

Complaints

General complaints or enquiries regarding privacy policies can be lodged to info.bestparadisehotel@sydney.edu.au or by direct phone calls to +61293512222.

Review of Privacy

BestMate Paradise will review and update its Privacy Policy periodically and have it published in this page whenever any new changes are made.
